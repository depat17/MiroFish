# PRD Writer Skill

## Skill Definition
The PRD Writer skill is designed to generate complete Product Requirements Documents (PRD) from feature recommendations and simulation results. It streamlines the process of documenting essential product features and requirements by utilizing insights derived from data and best practices in product management.

## Features:
1. **Feature Recommendations**: Automatically integrates and analyzes feature recommendations based on user feedback, market research, and competitive analysis.
2. **Simulation Results**: Incorporates simulation results to provide empirical evidence for product features, ensuring that every aspect of the PRD is data-driven.
3. **Comprehensive Structure**: Generates a structured document that includes:
   - **Introduction**: Overview of the product and objectives.
   - **Scope**: Defines the boundaries of the product and what it will encompass.
   - **Requirements**: Detailed requirements including functional, non-functional, and performance specifications.
   - **Acceptance Criteria**: Clearly defined criteria to measure if the product meets its goals.
   - **User Stories**: Captures end-user interactions and expectations.
   - **Mockups/Prototypes**: Linkage to visual aids for stakeholders’ understanding.
4. **Customization**: Allows users to customize the content based on stakeholders’ needs and specific project requirements.

## Usage:
To utilize the PRD Writer skill, users need to input relevant feature recommendations and simulation data. The system will transform these inputs into a well-defined PRD.

## Benefits:
- Reduces time spent on documentation.
- Ensures consistency and thoroughness across PRDs.
- Leverages data to make informed decisions for product development.

## Conclusion:
The PRD Writer skill is an essential tool for product teams seeking to enhance their documentation processes and ensure that all aspects of product requirements are meticulously captured and communicated.
