# Risk Detector Skill

## Overview
The Risk Detector skill is designed to assist in decision-making by cross-referencing proposed decisions against a timeline memory of past attempts and their outcomes. This skill aims to flag similarities, thereby enabling users to evaluate potential risks based on historical data.

## Skill Definition
- **Skill Name**: Risk Detector
- **Purpose**: To identify and highlight potential risks in proposed decisions by analyzing historical data.
- **Functionality**:
  - Collects and stores historical decision data, including outcomes.
  - Analyzes new proposals and compares them with past attempts.
  - Flags similar cases with outcomes to guide decision-making.

## Inputs
- Proposed decision data (e.g., decision description, context)
- Historical data (e.g., timeline of past decisions, outcomes)

## Outputs
- Risk assessment report highlighting potential risks based on historical comparisons.

## Use Cases
1. **Decision Support**: Helps individuals and teams make informed decisions by leveraging past experiences.
2. **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies recurring patterns in decision outcomes to provide actionable insights.

## Implementation Considerations
- Ensure robust data storage for historical decision records.
- Integrate with existing decision-making frameworks to enhance usability and effectiveness.

## Conclusion
The Risk Detector skill provides a crucial function in minimizing the risk of repeating past mistakes by drawing on a rich history of decision outcomes, ultimately leading to better-informed choices and strategic planning.
